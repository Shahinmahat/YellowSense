About Us

The present challenge revolves around the growing difficulty in finding reliable domestic help such as housekeepers, cooks, and caregivers. This imbalance has created a significant gap between the increasing demand for these services and the limited supply of qualified service providers. On the flip side, businesses within the service sector are grappling with the task of connecting their workforce with suitable employment opportunities. What is urgently needed is a platform that can efficiently bridge this divide, serving as a liaison between those seeking domestic services and those offering them. Such a platform would not only make it more convenient for households and individuals to access the assistance they require but also provide service providers with a streamlined method for finding job opportunities that align with their skills and qualifications. In doing so, it would not only help rebalance the domestic service industry but also enhance job satisfaction and stability for service providers while meeting the growing demand for these services in a more organized manner.
